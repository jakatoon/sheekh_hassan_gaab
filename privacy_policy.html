Introduction

Welcome to Sheekh Xasan Gaab Tafsiir. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information from and about you when you use our mobile application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed to be simple and focused on providing you with religious content. We do not require you to create an account or provide any direct personally identifiable information (like your name or email address). The information we collect is limited and primarily for functional and advertising purposes.

  • Usage Data: We automatically collect certain information through our third-party service providers, such as Google AdMob. This includes your device's advertising ID, IP address, device type, and interactions with ads.
  • Locally Stored Data: The app stores some data directly on your device for a better user experience. This data is not sent to us. This includes your list of favorite audio files, downloaded audio files, and your last played track.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To allow you to download audio files for offline listening and save your favorites.
  • To Support the App: To display advertisements which help us keep the application free for everyone. The data collected by our ad provider (Google AdMob) is used to show you more relevant ads.
  • To Improve User Experience: To understand how our app is used so we can improve its features and performance.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob as our advertising partner. AdMob is a third-party service that may collect and use information as described in its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policy to understand their data practices.

This application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
