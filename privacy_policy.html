Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Sheekh Xasan Gaab Tafseer" application ("we") handles your information. Our commitment is to protect your privacy. This app is designed to be a simple, offline-first audio player and does not collect any of your personal data.

Information We Collect

The "Sheekh Xasan Gaab Tafseer" application **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information** from its users. All data related to your usage of the app is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

The only data stored on your device by the app includes:

  • Downloaded Audio Files: When you download or stream an audio file, it is saved to your device's local storage to enable offline playback.
  • User Preferences: We store simple preferences, such as whether you have seen the welcome screen, a list of your favorite tracks, and the last audio file you played. This information is stored only on your device to improve your user experience.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. The locally stored data is used solely for the core functionality of the app, such as:

  • Providing offline access to audio lectures.
  • Allowing you to manage your favorite lectures.
  • Enabling you to resume playback from where you left off.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Internet: This permission is required to stream and download the audio files from the online source.
  • Storage (Read/Write): This permission is required to save the downloaded audio files to your device for offline access.

Data Security

All data, including downloaded audio files and preferences, is stored securely within the application's private directory on your device. This data is not accessible by other applications and is not transmitted over the internet.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the developer contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
